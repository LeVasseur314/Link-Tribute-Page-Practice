Link from Legend of Zelda

The hero of Hyrule

Here is a list of some of his greatest achievements

  • Skyward Sword - Formed the Goddess Sword into the Master Sword
  • Skyward Sword - Sealed away demise, the original demon king
  • Ocarina of Time - Sealed away Ganondorf for the first time
  • Majora's Mask - Destroyed the evil spirit sealed inside of Majora's Mask
  • Twilight Princess - Killed Ganondorf and saved the twilight realm
  • Link between Worlds - Saved Lorule from destruction by returning that kingdom's triforce
  • Breath of the Wild - Destroyed the spirit of Calamity Ganon that had trapped Zelda for 100 years

If you have time, you should read more about this amazing character on his Wikipedia entry.
